# Privacy Notice — Boulder Pain Relief

**Effective date:** March 2026
**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

---

## What we collect

When you use the pain-reporting tool, we collect:

- **Body map data** — which regions hurt, rated 0–10, and the size of each pain area (pinpoint / regional / diffuse)
- **Activity context** — optional free-text descriptions of what you were doing when pain occurred (e.g., "prolonged sitting at a computer")
- **Symptom modifiers** — what makes pain better or worse, and how long you've had it
- **Account information** — your email address and the name you choose to provide

We do **not** collect your location, device identifiers, or any browsing behavior outside this app.

---

## Why we collect it

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Pain map + activity notes | Generate a structured clinical assessment for your practitioner |
| Assessment results | Displayed to you immediately; stored so your practitioner can review your history |
| Email address | Account authentication and appointment matching |
| Full name | Display in practitioner dashboard (optional) |

---

## Who sees your data

- **You** — always, via your dashboard
- **Your practitioner** — the licensed massage therapist at Boulder Pain Relief sees your submitted reports and AI-generated assessments
- **Anthropic (Claude API)** — receives *anonymized* pain pattern data only (no name, email, or other identifiers) to generate clinical assessments. Anthropic's data processing is covered by a Business Associate Agreement (BAA).
- **Supabase** — hosts the database and authentication service. Covered by a BAA.

No data is sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or third parties for any purpose other than providing care.

---

## AI-generated assessments

The clinical assessments shown in the app are generated by an AI (Anthropic Claude). They are **clinical decision support tools for your practitioner — not medical diagnoses**. Your practitioner reviews all assessments before acting on them.

---

## Data retention

Pain reports are retained for the duration of your care relationship. You may request deletion of your records at any time by emailing contact@boulderpainrelief.com.

---

## Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256 via Supabase)
- Access controls ensure only you and your practitioner can view your records (enforced at the database level via Row Level Security)
- An audit log records who accessed which records, without storing PHI in the log itself
- Sessions automatically expire after 15 minutes of inactivity

---

## Your rights

You have the right to:
- Access a copy of your records
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your records
- Withdraw consent at any time (this will end your ability to use the app)

To exercise any of these rights, contact: **contact@boulderpainrelief.com**
